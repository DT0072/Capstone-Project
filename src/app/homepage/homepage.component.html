About Us

George Town is the colorful, multicultural capital of the Malaysian island of Penang. Once an important Straits of Malacca trading hub, the city is known for its British colonial buildings, Chinese shophouses and mosques. Beyond the old town, George Town is a modern city with skyscrapers and shopping malls. Verdant Penang Hill, with hiking and a funicular railway, overlooks it all.

Penang Hill Ultimate Travel Guide: Coolest things to See, Do & Eat on Penang's Famous Hill Station

Rising in the central part of the island, Penang Hill (known in Malay as “Bukit Bendera”, or Flag Hill) is an imposing 833-meters-high hill, and one of the unmissable things to do in Penang.

City Hall

Located at the westernmost point of Penang Island, this majestic building is a funky formula of Neo-Baroque and Palladian architectural styles, featuring colonnades, arched windows, and a sharp rooftop silhouette.
