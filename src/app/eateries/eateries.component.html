Duck Kway Chap At Kimberley Street

A word of caution if this will be your first time trying this dish - it doesn't appeal to everyone and takes some getting used to. Personally, I don't like it but my partner and our friends did. The dining environment is not attractive, clean or comfortable either. Because this is a hawker stall at the edge of a busy road.
Tip: You can choose to sit at the opposite dessert shop if you get drinks from them.

However, if you are a fan of kway chap or just have a curious appetite, the broth is thick and flavorful. The dish has duck meat, braised eggs and various pig parts according to preference (e.g., pork intestines or pig ears). Other food that we recommended here are char koay teow, loh bak and fried oysters with egg (oh chien).

Location:
135, Lebuh Kimberley, George Town, 10100 George Town, Pulau Pinang

Opening Hours: 2pm - 10pm
Closed On: Thursday

Penang Road Famous Laksa

This famous laksa shop serves lai fun noodles in a sardine broth with exceptional depth and flavours alongside an array of condiments. For those with bigger appetites, order char koay teow with duck egg to go with the laksa.

Location:
5 Lebuh Keng Kwee, George Town, 10100, Malaysia.

Opening Hours:
Monday & Tuesday: 9AM - 5PM Thursday- Sunday: 9AM - 5:30 PM

Closed On: Wednesday
